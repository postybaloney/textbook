Grading and policies

  • Assignments: Weekly assignments will be posted on the web page, and they are due before the following lecture (at whatever time we decide). The assignments will be given and written in the form of Google Colab Python notebooks. The completed assignment should be in the form of a Python Notebook(.ipynb) and submitted online to the attendance form. Group discussions about the assignments are highly encouraged, but each student must write his/her own solutions and computer codes and not copy them from anyone else.

  • Project: There will be 1 programming project due by the end of the semester. The projects will be given and written in the form of Jupyter notebooks. The completed project as well as all previous homework should be uploaded to the attendance form as an ipynb-file. Group discussions about the project is required, and only one student from each group should submit the final project but each student should submit all previous homeworks and a note of the group they were in. Late submissions will not be accepted, and might result in a retainment of the club fee

  • Special arrangements: If you are a student with a disability registered by the Disabled Student Program (DSP) on UCB campus and if you require special arrangements during exams, you must provide the DSP document and make arrangements via email or office hours at least 10 days prior to each exam, explaining your circumstances and what special arrangements need to be done. Also see your GSI as soon as possible to make arrangements for the homework/quizzes.
